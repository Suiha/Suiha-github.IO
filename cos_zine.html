Curse of Strahd Zine



Curse of Strahd


Curse of Strahd is a Dungeons & Dragons (5th edition) gothic horror adventure where players are trapped in Barovia, a mysterious land surrounded by mist. Throughout the adventure, players will learn more about and confront the titular villain, Strahd von Zarovich, who is both a wizard and a vampire.

I created a series of images related to an upcoming Curse of Strahd campaign I will be running and put them together into a zine, or small magazine. The zine itself contains 8 images, 5 for the 5 player characters, 2 for a major landmark, and 1 for an important non-player character (NPC.
