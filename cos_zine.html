Curse of Strahd Zine



Curse of Strahd (CoS) is a Dungeons & Dragons (5th edition) gothic horror adventure where players are trapped in Barovia, a mysterious land surrounded by mist. Throughout the adventure, players will learn more about and confront the titular villain, Strahd von Zarovich, who is both a wizard and a vampire.

A zine is “a noncommercial often homemade or online publication usually devoted to specialized and often unconventional subject matter” (Merriam Webster), similar to a magazine.

I created a series of images related to an upcoming Curse of Strahd campaign I will be running and put them together into a zine\. The zine itself contains 8 images, 5 for the 5 player characters, 2 for a important non-player-characters (NPCs), and 1 for an significant landmark (NPC).





Creating the Zine


To draw these digital paintings, I used Clip Studio Paint (CSP), a digital drawing program similar to Photoshop. CSP also offered features such as access to free custom tools, patterns, and 3D models made by the CSP community to augment my process.

I then combined all the images into one using Electric Zine Maker, which formats images using different templates for zines.

Character Illustrations


I dedicated 5 of the 8 total digital paintings to each of my 5 player characters. These all represent aspects about their character that stood out to me the most.

Thematic Illustrations


I dedicated 3 of the 8 total digital paintings to content from the original Curse of Strahd adventure. These illustrations reflect what I perceive to be the most iconic parts of the game.
