Curse of Strahd Zine

Warning: Some spoilers for Curse of Strahd


Thematic Illustrations


Curse of Strahd takes place in Barovia, and players are unable to venture away from it. As such, prominent certain landscapes and characters will be reoccuring features in the adventure. Since I dedicated 5 of my 8 digital paintings to my 5 player characters, that left only 3 images for Curse of Strahd itself. As such, I chose to highlight what I believed to be the most iconic part of the adventure.



Title Page: Strahd


Curse of Strahd's titular villain, Strahd von Zarovich, is not a one-time final boss. Through the adventure, he is a constant presence in the main characters' journey; not only will he find ways to interact with them in-person, but he also has spies throughout Barovia. He can also spy on the party himself through magical means.

So the title page simply depicts two pairs of red eyes - Strahd's eyes - lurking in the darkness, staring out at you.



Inner Page: Castle Ravenloft


Castle Ravenloft is a location rife with history and a key location for the adventure - not only is it the main place where the players can find Strahd, but it is also where the event that led to Barovia's current state occurred. Up on a cliff, not far from the first settlement the players enter, Castle Ravenloft is a clear landmark in Barovia.

I chose to depict Castle Ravenloft at night, the only time when the sky can be clear. The full moon casts an oppressive light over the land and the castle looms over everything below it.



Back Page: Ireena


Ireena Kolyana is another important non-player character in the adventure - in the first area, the players are asked by her brother to escort Ireena to a safe location, as Strahd has recently shown interest in making her one of his consorts, which is a fate not many would wish for. In reality, Strahd wants to marry Ireena, as she is the latest reincarnation of his old love, Tatyana.

In her first life, Tatyana rejected Strahd for his younger brother. Strahd, after being corrupted by Dark Powers, murdered his brother to become an immortal vampire. Horrified, Tatyana threw herself off a cliff to escape him. However, now that Strahd's new powers trapped Barovia from the outside world, her soul was also trapped within it, and so she would reincarnate several times until her most recent one as Ireena.

In contrast with the other illustration of Castle Ravenloft, the point of view for this piece is from the cliff where Castle Ravenloft sits during the day, at the scene where Tatyana died.
